# Welcome to Give

Give is a modern fundraising platform built for nonprofits that believe in transparency. Instead of hiding fees behind donor tip prompts or charging expensive monthly subscriptions, Give charges a simple, flat platform fee.

## Why Give?

Other platforms claim to be "free" but charge donors 15-17% in hidden tips. Or they charge nonprofits 3%+ in platform fees plus monthly subscriptions. Give is different:

- **No donor tips** — Donors know exactly where their money goes
- **No monthly fees** — You only pay when you raise money
- **Automatic payouts** — Daily or weekly deposits to your bank, no manual withdrawals
- **Full API** — Build custom integrations from day one
