# Pricing

Give charges a simple platform fee on donations processed. No monthly subscriptions. No setup fees. No hidden costs.

## Payment Processing Fees

These are Stripe's fees, passed through at cost. Give does not mark up processing fees.

| Payment Method | Fee |
|---------------|-----|
| Credit / Debit Card | 2.2% + $0.30 |
| ACH / Bank Transfer | 0.8% (capped at $5) |
| Apple Pay / Google Pay | Same as card |

<Tip>
  **Save with ACH:** A $1,000 donation costs $22.30 to process via card but only $5.00 via ACH. Encourage ACH for larger gifts to save your donors and your organization money.
</Tip>

## Cover the Fee

Donors can optionally cover both the processing fee and Give's platform fee at checkout. When they do, your nonprofit receives 100% of the intended donation amount.

### Example: $100 Donation (Basic Tier, Card Payment)

| | Without Cover Fee | With Cover Fee |
|--|---|---|
| **Donor pays** | $100.00 | $103.62 |
| **Processing fee** | $2.50 | $2.58 |
| **Platform fee (1%)** | $1.00 | $1.04 |
| **Nonprofit receives** | $96.50 | **$100.00** |

## Compared to Alternatives

| Platform | What Nonprofits Pay | What Donors Pay |
|----------|-------------------|-----------------|
| **Give Basic** | **1% platform fee** | Nothing extra (or optional fee coverage) |
| **Give Pro** | **2% platform fee** | Nothing extra (or optional fee coverage) |
| Zeffy | 0% | 15-17% default "voluntary" tip |
| Givebutter (tips on) | 0% | ~15% default tip + processing |
| Givebutter (tips off) | 3% + processing | Nothing extra |
| Classy / GoFundMe Pro | $299/mo + processing | Nothing extra |
| Donorbox | 2.95% + processing | Nothing extra |

<Info>
  On Zeffy and Givebutter, the "tip" is pre-selected at 15-17% and requires multiple clicks to remove. Many donors don't realize they're paying it. Give believes in transparent pricing — for nonprofits **and** donors.
</Info>
