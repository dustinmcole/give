# Peer-to-Peer Fundraising

<Note>
  Peer-to-peer fundraising is a **Pro tier** feature, coming soon.
</Note>

Let your supporters create their own fundraising pages that roll up to your campaign goal. Birthday fundraisers, walkathons, challenge campaigns — your community does the outreach while you track the results.

## Planned Features

- Individual fundraiser pages with personal stories and photos
- Team pages with aggregate tracking
- Leaderboards for competitive campaigns
- Social sharing tools
- Automatic rollup to parent campaign totals
- Supporter contact data available to the nonprofit
