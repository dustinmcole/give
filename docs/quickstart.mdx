# Quickstart

Follow these steps to start accepting donations with Give.

## 1. Create Your Organization

Sign up at [app.give.dev](https://app.give.dev/signup) with your nonprofit's details.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter your organization details">
    Provide your nonprofit name, EIN (for 501(c)(3) verification), and contact information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your bank account">
    Complete Stripe Connect onboarding to link your bank account for payouts. This takes about 2 minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your plan">
    Start on the **Basic tier** (1% platform fee) or upgrade to **Pro** (2%) for advanced features.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## 2. Create a Campaign

From your dashboard, click **Create Campaign** and configure:

- **Title** — Your campaign name (e.g., "Annual Fund 2026")
- **Goal** — Optional fundraising target to display a progress thermometer
- **Suggested amounts** — Pre-set donation amounts for donors to choose from
- **Recurring options** — Enable monthly, quarterly, or annual giving

## 3. Share Your Donation Form

Every campaign gets a shareable donation page and an embeddable widget.

### Hosted Page
Share the link directly:
```
https://give.dev/donate/your-campaign-slug
```

### Embeddable Widget
Drop this snippet into any website:
```html
<script src="https://cdn.give.dev/widget.js"></script>
<div data-give-campaign="your-campaign-id"></div>
```

### QR Code
Download a QR code from your campaign settings for print materials and events.

## 4. Start Receiving Donations

That's it. Donations flow through Stripe, fees are calculated transparently, and funds are automatically deposited to your bank on your chosen schedule (daily or weekly).

<Info>
  **Processing fees** (Stripe's 2.2% + $0.30 for cards, 0.8% capped at $5 for ACH) are separate from Give's platform fee. Donors can optionally cover all fees so your nonprofit receives 100% of the intended donation.
</Info>
