# Stripe Integration

Give uses Stripe Connect to process all payments. Each nonprofit gets their own Stripe Express account, giving them direct access to their funds while Give manages the platform experience.

## How It Works

1. **Onboarding** — When a nonprofit signs up, they complete Stripe's Express onboarding (identity verification, bank account linking). Takes ~2 minutes.
2. **Donations** — When a donor gives, Give creates a PaymentIntent on the nonprofit's connected Stripe account.
3. **Fees** — Stripe processing fees and Give's platform fee are automatically calculated and separated.
4. **Payouts** — Funds are automatically deposited to the nonprofit's bank account on their chosen schedule (daily or weekly).

## Nonprofit Rates

Give qualifies for Stripe's nonprofit processing rates:

| Method | Rate |
|--------|------|
| Credit/Debit | 2.2% + $0.30 |
| ACH | 0.8% (capped at $5) |
| Apple Pay / Google Pay | Same as card |

## Dashboard

Nonprofits can access their Stripe Express dashboard to view:
- Individual transaction details
- Payout history and schedule
- Dispute/chargeback management
